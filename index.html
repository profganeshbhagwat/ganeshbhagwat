Mr. Ganesh Bhagwat is an experienced academician with over 10+ years of teaching experience in the field of Computer Science and Information Technology. He possesses strong expertise in delivering high-quality education in subjects such as Big Data Analytics, Web Programming, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning. He has a deep academic interest in Big Data Analytics, where he actively contributes through teaching, research, and practical implementations. His teaching methodology focuses on bridging the gap between theoretical concepts and real-world applications, ensuring that students gain both conceptual clarity and hands-on experience. Mr. Bhagwat has made significant contributions to academia as a co-author of 7 textbooks, which are used as reference materials by students and educators. In addition, he has published 5 research papers in reputed journals, reflecting his commitment to continuous learning and knowledge sharing in emerging technologies. He is actively involved in MCA (Master of Computer Applications) programs, where he not only teaches core and advanced subjects but also plays a key role in project guidance, student mentoring, and academic development. His mentorship helps students develop technical skills, research orientation, and industry readiness. With a passion for innovation and education, Mr. Bhagwat continuously updates his knowledge in cutting-edge technologies and encourages students to explore areas like data science, machine learning, and big data solutions.
